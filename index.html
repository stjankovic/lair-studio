ABOUT

Lair Studio specialise is residential & hospitality interios on the Northern Beaches, Sydney.
Director, Rachel Mutton, graduated with a degree in interior design from the University of the
Arts London in 2008. With many years experience in residential & hospitality interiors in both
Europe and Australia we work with you directly to understand your wants and needs to create
an interior concept unique to you. We provide a range of services catering for both large
and small scale projects including: floorplan re-design, colour consultation, materials & finish
specification, furniture selection & layouts, kitchen, bathroom, & bespoke joinery design.
